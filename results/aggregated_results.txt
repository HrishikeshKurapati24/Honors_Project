# Evaluation Report: Cancer Drug Response Prediction Models

## 1. Executive Summary

Across all evaluated metrics—AUC, AUPR, F1, and Accuracy—the **Transcriptomics + Epigenomics (Methylation + Chromatin Profiling + Gene Expression)** combination with the **GINConv** architecture consistently outperforms other setups.

This model successfully captures gene-level activity together with the regulatory context (via methylation and chromatin accessibility), providing a more complete picture of drug sensitivity mechanisms.

**Key Findings:**
- **Best Performer:** Transcriptomics + Epigenomics (AUC: 0.9516, F1: 0.8094)
- **Runner Up:** Transcriptomics + Proteomics (AUC: 0.9471)
- **Insight:** Combinations including *all* omics data or adding pathway scores did not outperform biologically focused subsets, suggesting that adding too many layers can introduce noise.

## 3. Detailed Experiment Results

### A. Novel / Modified Architectures (Our Contributions)

#### 1. Transcriptomics + Epigenomics (Winner)
*Configuration:* Methylation + Chromatin Profiling + Gene Expression
- **Architecture:** GINConv
- **Time:** ~198s
- **Metrics:** AUC: 0.9516 | AUPR: 0.8979 | F1: 0.8094 | ACC: 0.8874
- **Note:** Achieved lower training loss and high stability.

#### 2. Transcriptomics + Proteomics
- **Architecture:** GINConv
- **Metrics:** AUC: 0.9518 | AUPR: 0.8995 | F1: 0.8115 | ACC: 0.8886
- **Note:** Slightly higher raw metrics but showed higher final training loss, indicating potential overfitting compared to the Epigenomics model.

#### 3. Ablation Studies (Model Variations)
- **GraphSAGE:** AUC 0.9502 (Very competitive)
- **GCNConv:** AUC 0.9465
- **GATConv:** AUC 0.9433 (Slower convergence: ~397s)
- **FC Layers Only (No Attention):** AUC 0.9468
- **VAE Dimensionality Reduction:** AUC 0.9465

### B. Baseline Models

#### 1. GraphCDR (Reference State-of-the-Art)
- **Single Seed:** AUC: 0.9436 | AUPR: 0.8776 | F1: 0.7970
- **Multi-Seed (Mean of 5):** AUC: 0.9387 ± 0.0019
- **Performance:** Strong baseline, but outperformed by our modified Epigenomics architectures.

#### 2. GCLM-CDR
- **Single Seed:** AUC: 0.8063 | AUPR: 0.6712 | F1: 0.6272
- **Performance:** Significantly lower performance compared to GraphCDR and our Modified models.
